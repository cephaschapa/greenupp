Intellignose is a diagnostics AI tool designed to diagnose and determine the state of crops. It has the ability to prescribe precise vetenary or pesticide recommendation and dosage. Depending on your location, Intellignose can also recommend you to a nearby vet and available prescribed pesticides online i.e Green Market.
